NatureSense

A Plant and Animal Identification Application

Make Observations and Learn About Nature

Utilizing image recognition technology, easily identify different organisms you discover.

NatureSense allows you to identify plants and animals and learn more information about them, helping spread biodiversity knowledge and aiding in conservation. The identification pages are supplemented with general descriptions of the observed organism, advice when encountering it, and other important information. NatureSense records the observations you make and you can keep track of the plants and animals you have discovered. Users can share their findings and connect with others.

Explore your environment and take a picture or select a photo from your photo library to identify wildlife.

  • Identify animals and plants
  • Get insights on your observations
  • Connect with like minded individuals
